### Ingredients:

- butter: 160 g [about 1 stick]
- walnut: 100 g [~1 cup]
- sugar: 120 g [~1.25 cup]
- egg: 2
- flour: 100 g [~1 cup]
- vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
- green tea powder: 2 tablespoons
 
### Cooking directions

1. Leave butter out until it turn soft naturally.
2. Cut walnut into small pieces. [or not, I don't.]
3. Mix Butter and sugar until it turns whitish.
4. Add mixed egg little by little, then add vanilla extract.
5. Mix flour and matcha tea powder and strain it through a sieve.
6. Mix (4) and (5) and put it in a sheeted pan.
7. Bake the dough for 55 min. at 300°.

Let it cool…or try it while its warm! Enjoy!
